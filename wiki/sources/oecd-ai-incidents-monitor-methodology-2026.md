# Overview and methodology of the AI Incidents and Hazards Monitor

**来源**：OECD.AI
**URL**：https://oecd.ai/en/incidents-methodology

## 摘要

OECD AI Incidents and Hazards Monitor tracks AI incidents and hazards from reputable international news sources to provide an evidence base for policy discussions. Its methodology defines incidents/hazards, classifies news events, and discloses limits such as reliance on third-party reporting.

## 关键贡献

- 将 AI incidents 和 hazards 做成可持续监控对象，而不是零散 anecdote。
- 明确 AIM 是证据起点，不能代表 OECD 对每条第三方报道的独立验证。
- 公开来源监控适合观察风险模式，但组织仍需要自己的 production monitoring、用户报告和审计证据。
- 对本 vault 的意义：AI incident response 要同时吸收外部 incident intelligence 和内部 trace/eval evidence。
